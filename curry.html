Stephen Curry

Overview

Stephen Curry has played 14 seasons for the Warriors. He has averaged 24.5 points, 6.5 assists and 4.7 rebounds in 864 regular-season games. He's generally known as one of the, if not THE, best shooter(s) in NBA history.

Team(s) played on

Stephen Curry was drafted as the 7th overall pick in the 2009 NBA draft after he played one stellar year of college basketball at Davidson College. He went to the Golden State Warriors and has played there for 14 seasons, not once going to another team.

Notable Achievements

  • 4x NBA Champion
  • NBA Finals MVP: 2022
  • Western Conference Finals MVP: 2022
  • 1st All-time in 3 pointers made in NBA history
  • 2x NBA Most Valuable Player: 2015, 2016 (With the only unanimous MVP selection in league history in Curry's 2016 MVP)
  • 9x NBA All-Star: 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2021, 2022, 2023 (NBA All-Star Game MVP: 2022)
  • 8x All-NBA Selection (4x All-NBA First Team, 3x All-NBA Second Team, 1x All-NBA Third Team)
  • All-Rookie First Team
  • 7x NBA three-point field goals leader: 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2021, 2022
  • 2x NBA scoring leader: 2016, 2021
  • NBA regular season record for made three-pointers (402)
  • Warriors Franchise leader in points, assists, steals, three-point field goals made, assists in playoffs, three-point field goals in playoffs, steals in playoffs, points in Playoffs
  • 1st place all-time for three-pointers made in NBA history.
  • Lead the Warriors to a 73-9 record in the NBA
